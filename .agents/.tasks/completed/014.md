# Implement or remove quiet and verbose flags

## Summary

Review issue 14. --quiet and --verbose were exposed but not consulted. After analysis, `ahm` has no natural "noise spectrum" to dial — output *is* the result, with no progress bars, debug lines, or informational chatter to suppress or amplify. Reserved no-op flags add unnecessary surface area to `--help`, the options struct, and docs. Removed both flags from the options struct, flag registration, and `docs/cli.md`.

## Acceptance Notes

- [x] Removed `quiet` and `verbose` fields from `options` struct in `cli.go`
- [x] Removed `--quiet` and `--verbose` persistent flag registrations in `cli.go`
